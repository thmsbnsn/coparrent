# Tester Accounts Template

Use the live credentials in `tester-accounts.local.md`, which is intentionally gitignored.

## Account Label
- email: `example@example.com`
- password: `replace-me`
- created_at: `YYYY-MM-DD`
- verification_method: `email | sms | WhatsApp | none`
- verification_owner: `who receives the code`
- has_account: `false`
- role_target: `parent | co-parent | third-party | child | admin | paid-subscriber`
- subscription_state: `free | power | trial | canceled`
- onboarding_state: `not started | partial | complete`
- last_used: `never`
- notes: `What this account is reserved for`
